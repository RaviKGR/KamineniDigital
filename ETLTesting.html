ETL Testing

Ensure data quality and integrity with our ETL Testing course. Learn how to verify and validate data extraction, transformation, and loading processes, critical for maintaining reliable data workflows.

Key Topics Covered

  • Introduction to ETL and Data Warehousing Concepts
  • ETL Tools and Techniques (Informatica, SSIS, Talend)
  • Writing Test Cases for ETL Processes
  • Data Validation, Verification, and Reconciliation
  • Automation of ETL Testing
  • Best Practices and Real-World Testing Scenarios

Course Duration

5 Weeks

Who Should Attend

QA testers, data analysts, IT professionals involved in data migration and ETL processes.

Outcome

Acquire practical knowledge in ETL testing, ensuring data quality and accuracy in your organization’s data management workflows
